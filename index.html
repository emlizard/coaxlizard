Mathematical Formulas

Characteristic Impedance (Z₀)

\[ Z_0 = \frac{60}{\sqrt{\epsilon_r}} \ln\!\left(\frac{D}{d}\right) \]

Outer Conductor Diameter (D)

For a target impedance Z₀:
\[ D = d \cdot \exp\!\left(\frac{Z_{0,\text{target}} \sqrt{\epsilon_r}}{60}\right) \]

Capacitance per Unit Length (C)

\[ C = \frac{2\pi\epsilon_0\epsilon_r}{\ln(D/d)} \quad \text{[F/m]} \]
